FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear during a spa treatment?

We provide complimentary spa robes, slippers, and disposable garments for all treatments. You will be fully draped and your modesty is always respected throughout every session. Simply arrive comfortably and let us take care of everything.

How early should I arrive before my appointment?

We recommend arriving 15–20 minutes before your scheduled time. This allows you to check in, complete your wellness consultation, change, and begin unwinding in our tranquil relaxation lounge before your treatment starts.

Do you offer treatments for pregnant guests?

Yes, we offer specially adapted prenatal massage and facial treatments that are safe and deeply beneficial during pregnancy. Please inform us when booking so we can assign a therapist certified in prenatal care and prepare a pregnancy-safe treatment plan for you.

What is your cancellation & rescheduling policy?

We ask that cancellations or rescheduling requests be made at least 24 hours before your appointment. Cancellations with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a 50% charge. We understand that life happens — simply reach out and our team will do their best to accommodate you.

Can I book a private room for a couple's treatment?

Absolutely. Our exclusive Couple Bliss Suite is available for bookings and includes side-by-side treatment beds, a private Jacuzzi, champagne service, and a curated aromatherapy ambience. Advance booking is required for suite reservations.

Are all your products organic and cruelty-free?

Yes. Every product used in our treatments is 100% organic, sustainably sourced, free from parabens and synthetic fragrances, and certified cruelty-free. We are proud partners of several ethical wellness brands and our entire retail range is available for purchase in-spa.
